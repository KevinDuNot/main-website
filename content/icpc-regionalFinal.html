Welcome to ICPC

The International Collegiate Programming Contest is an algorithmic programming contest for university students. Teams of three work collaboratively to solve the most real-world problems, fostering creativity, innovation, and the ability to perform under pressure. Quite simply, it is the oldest, largest, and most prestigious programming contest in the world.

Event for the day
